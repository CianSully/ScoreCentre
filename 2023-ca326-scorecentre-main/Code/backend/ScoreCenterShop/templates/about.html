About ScoreCenter

Welcome to ScoreCenter, our NBA website, where you can find the latest news, fixtures, results and stats for the National Basketball Association. Our website was created as a third year project for college by Cian Sullivan and Leigh Reilly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide NBA fans with the most up-to-date and comprehensive coverage of their favorite sport. We strive to deliver accurate and timely news, in-depth game analysis, and detailed player and team statistics.

Our Features

  • Live news feed with the latest stories from around the league
  • Complete schedule of NBA games with information about each matchup
  • Real-time game results
  • Player statistics for the current season
  • Team wins for a season dating back to 2009
